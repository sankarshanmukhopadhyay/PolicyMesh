# v0.11.0 — Governance Substrate, Observability, and Operational Hardening

This release is a substantial increment across governance, policy operations, observability, and deployment posture.
It establishes a stronger substrate for village policy management without claiming that every roadmap item is complete.

## Highlights

### Governance & Policy Evolution
- Weighted and role-based quorum support is scaffolded alongside M-of-N policy update handling.
- Policy diff tooling and machine-readable change summaries are available.
- Versioning and rollback semantics are present, with activation-time support introduced in the governance surface.

### Distributed Policy Substrate
- Signed policy feed manifests and integrity metadata are present.
- Pagination hooks and reconciliation or fork reporting primitives have been added.
- Trust anchor registry workflows for register, rotate, and revoke are available in the codebase.

### Assurance & Observability
- Audit export endpoints and CLI (`links audit export`) support JSON and CSV.
- SHA-256 digest generation and optional digest signing with the node key are available.
- Drift checking and transparency-oriented policy publishing are present as operational building blocks.

### Operational Hardening
- Filesystem write paths have been tightened for safer operation.
- Deployment guidance is available under `docs/deploy/` and a container baseline is included.
- A minimal load-oriented helper script is available under `scripts/`.

### Security & Risk Controls
- Replay protection for already-ingested bundle IDs is present.
- Village-level quota and denial handling have been introduced.
- Signature entries now carry `alg` metadata as part of cryptographic agility groundwork.

### Ecosystem & Interoperability
- External registry import/export commands are present.
- A Python HTTP client wrapper and SDK surface are included.
- Example snapshot material and governance conformance coverage are included.

## Upgrade notes
- If you enable digest signing, set `LINKS_NODE_SIGNING_KEY_B64` (base64 Ed25519 seed).
- Public policy endpoint exposure is opt-in via `LINKS_PUBLIC_POLICY=1` or `policy.public_policy_endpoint=true`.
